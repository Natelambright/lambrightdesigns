About

Bold.
Expressive.
Always evolving.

I'm a Virginia-based digital artist and designer with an A.A.S. in Graphic and Media Design from Virginia Peninsula Community College (graduating May 2026). My work focuses on typography, vector illustration, and motion graphics, with additional creative interests in photography and visual storytelling.

I primarily create using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and After Effects. My style blends chaotic, playful, dark, and unconventional influences. As an artist living with cerebral palsy, I see art as both a creative outlet and a form of freedom — allowing me to communicate ideas, emotions, and identity in ways that feel unrestricted.
